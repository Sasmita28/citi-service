Kansas City 311 Dashboard

There are many issues that arise every day in the city of Kansas City that need addressing.
A project was undertaken to understand the frequency of the problems as well as how long they take to be closed.

Data was collected from the city website https://data.kcmo.org/311/311-Call-Center-Service-Requests/7at3-sxhp and investigated using a set of filters. The filters are as follows:

  • Year: This is the year an issue tag was created.
  • Status: This is the progress of the issue resolution.
  • Source: This is the method by which the city was notified of the issue.
  • Department: This is the sector of the city government that deals with the issue.

Charts were created to look at the average days_to_close time as well as the amount of calls lodged and the breakdown by department for each set of filter values.
